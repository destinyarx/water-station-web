# AI Agent Prompt: Implement Privacy Policy and Terms System for Water Refilling Station Management App

You are an AI coding agent working inside an existing Next.js web application.

Your task is to implement a simple but professional Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions system for a medium-sized free-to-use Water Refilling Station Management System.

Do not build an enterprise-level legal system.

Follow the existing project architecture, UI framework, routing conventions, and component patterns.

---

# Application Context

The application is:

A Water Refilling Station Management System that helps business owners and staff manage:

- Purified water businesses
- Mineral water businesses
- Water delivery operations
- Customer records
- Sales tracking
- Expense monitoring
- Staff accounts
- Business documents
- Transaction history

The system is mainly used by:

1. Water refilling station owners
2. Authorized staff members

# Legal Requirements

The legal pages should consider:

- Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012)
- National Privacy Commission (NPC) guidance

The documents should explain:

- What information is collected
- Why information is collected
- How information is protected
- User rights
- Data retention
- Contact information

Avoid complicated legal wording.

The application is a free medium-sized SaaS-style business tool.
